WHAT IS MARCH MATHNESS??

March Mathness is a single-elimination mathematics tournament/festival inspired by the NCAA basketball tournaments and is held in March and April. Its goal is to foster and encourage collaborative problem-solving for students of all mathematical backgrounds.
