REGISTER FOR MARCH MATHNESS!

Ready to register for March Mathness? Gather a team of one to four Timberline High School Students and complete the registration form by the end of the day on March 9, 2026. (NOTE: You may have noticed you can have a 'team' of one participant. March Mathness participants are certainly encouraged to collaborate with others, but in the spirit of inclusion, 'lone-wolf' competitors are welcome.)

While you're at it, help spread the word by printing the informational flyer and sharing the word! See Mr. Fogdall in room 213 with any questions. Have fun forming teams, and get ready for the Mathness to start March 11, 2026!
